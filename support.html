Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Open RepCam, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Ensure you are logged into the same Apple ID used for the initial purchase.

Are my photos private?
Yes. RepCam uses on-device processing. We never upload your photos to any servers.
