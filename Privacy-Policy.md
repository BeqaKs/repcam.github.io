Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 28.04.2026

RepCam (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is a gym photography app designed to help users capture, edit, save, and share physique photos.

Information We Collect
RepCam does not require an account. We do not sell your personal information and we do not use third-party advertising trackers.

RepCam may access the following data only when needed for app features:

Camera access: Used to show the live camera preview and capture photos.
Photo library access: Used to save edited photos and load photos you choose to view, edit, compare, or share.
Purchase information: Apple provides purchase status for subscriptions or lifetime purchases so we can unlock Pro features.
App settings: Preferences such as selected filters, onboarding status, pose coach settings, and purchase state may be stored locally on your device.
iCloud/CloudKit data: If enabled by the system, RepCam may sync app data such as session metadata and saved photo references through Apple iCloud.
Photos and Image Processing
Photos and edits are processed on your device. RepCam does not upload your photos to our servers. When you save or share a photo, it is handled by Apple’s Photos and sharing systems.

Payments
Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. We may share limited information only when required to operate app features, comply with law, or process purchases through Apple.

Children’s Privacy
RepCam is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices
You can manage camera, photo library, iCloud, and notification permissions in iOS Settings. You can manage subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.
