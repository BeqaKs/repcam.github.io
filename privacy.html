Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2026

RepCam (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is a gym photography app designed to help users capture, edit, save, and share physique photos. We prioritize user privacy and minimize data collection.

Information We Access

RepCam does not require an account. We do not sell your personal information and we do not use third-party advertising trackers.

RepCam may access the following data only when needed for app features:

  • Camera access: Used to show the live camera preview and capture photos.
  • Photo library access: Used to save edited photos and load photos you choose to view, edit, compare, or share.
  • Purchase information: Apple provides purchase status for subscriptions or lifetime purchases so we can unlock Pro features. We do not receive or store credit card details.
  • App settings: Preferences such as selected filters, onboarding status, and purchase state may be stored locally on your device.

Analytics

We use Aptabase, a privacy-focused analytics service, to understand basic product usage and improve RepCam. Analytics events are limited to generic app interactions such as app launch, onboarding completion, opening the camera, opening the gallery, viewing the paywall, tapping purchase or restore, and turning optional features on or off.

We do not send photos, videos, names, email addresses, body measurements, health information, precise location, notes, or any other sensitive personal content to Aptabase.

Aptabase may receive basic technical information such as app version, device type, operating system, and country-level region as part of analytics processing. Analytics is not used for advertising tracking and is not used to track you across apps or websites.

Photos and Image Processing

Photos and edits are processed on your device. RepCam does not upload your photos to our servers. When you save or share a photo, it is handled by Apple’s Photos and sharing systems.

Payments

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card information. Apple provides purchase status so RepCam can unlock Pro features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information only when required to operate app features, process purchases through Apple, use privacy-focused analytics through Aptabase, comply with law, or protect our rights.

Children’s Privacy

RepCam is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can manage camera and photo library permissions at any time through the iOS Settings app. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.
