Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2026

RepCam (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is a gym photography app designed to help users capture, edit, save, and share physique photos. We prioritize user privacy and minimize data collection.

Information We Collect

RepCam does not require an account to function. We do not sell your personal information and we do not use third-party advertising trackers.

RepCam may access the following data only when needed for app features:

  • Camera access: Used to show the live camera preview and capture photos.
  • Photo library access: Used to save edited photos and load photos you choose to view, edit, compare, or share.
  • Purchase information: Apple provides purchase status for subscriptions or lifetime purchases so we can unlock Pro features. We do not store or receive credit card details.
  • App settings: Preferences such as selected filters, onboarding status, and purchase state are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud/CloudKit data: If enabled, RepCam may sync app metadata such as session settings through Apple iCloud.

Analytics

We use Aptabase, a privacy-first, anonymized analytics platform, to help us understand how RepCam is used. This allows us to improve features like filter performance and app stability without compromising your identity.

  • Anonymous Usage: We track events such as app launches, feature engagement (e.g., activating specific Metal filters), and session duration.
  • No PII: Aptabase does not collect personally identifiable information (PII), names, emails, or precise location data. It does not track you across other apps or websites.
  • Technical Data: We may collect basic device information (device model and OS version) to ensure compatibility.

Photos and Image Processing

All image processing happens locally on your device. RepCam does not upload your photos to our servers. When you save or share a photo, it is handled exclusively by Apple’s native Photos and sharing systems.

Payments

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Your financial information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We only receive a status token to unlock your Pro features.

Children’s Privacy

RepCam is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can manage camera, photo library, iCloud, and notification permissions at any time through the iOS Settings app. You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings.
